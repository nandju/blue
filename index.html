World Oceans Day in Liberia

Last year, we welcomed volunteers from Liberia and worked with them on several projects. After their wonderful experience in Côte d’Ivoire, they decided to impact their community as well. On June 8, World Oceans Day, they visited communities living by the sea to raise awareness about good practices to adopt to protect our environment from plastic pollution. We are proud of their commitment and encourage each of us to adopt the right actions.

Non classé . 1 Comment . 22 juillet 2022
TREE PLANTING

WE MUST TAKE ADVANTAGE OF THIS RAINY SEASON TO HAVE AN ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT. Hello dear Blue Makers, we all know that we are currently in the rainy season and this is the best time to plant trees. So, we have initiated the operation one BLUE Maker one tree. This very special activity aims to create a link between each of us and the environment. It consists of planting a tree🌳 of your choice (in front of your house or in your city.) And then making a publication on Facebook, LinkedIn or Instagram in which he/she will say his/her attachment and the choice of this tree in particular. For example, the one who decides to plant a mango tree, will have to explain to us why he/she chose this tree personally and his/her attachment to it.

Blog . 1 Comment . 28 mai 2022
RECYCL’DAY

As part of its second activity called "RECYCL'DAY", the Blue Makers team organized this Saturday, May 28, 2022 at the Félix Houphouet-Boigny University to collect and recycle plastic waste. We made art objects from the collected plastic waste. Fighting pollution while giving a second life to our waste was the mission of the day. We would like to thank the German cooperation #GIZ, the German Embassy Abidjan and Exclusiv in who were kind enough to take part in the activity, a sign of their involvement in the implementation of the sustainable development goals. We also thank Mrs. Edith Brou for her advice and support which helped to hold this activity. So, are you ready for the next activity? #imabluemaker #environment #plasticpollution
